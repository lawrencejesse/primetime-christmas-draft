How This Works

Welcome to the first annual Prime Time Christmas Colt Draft. Grab a Baileys, pick your ponies, and let's see who knows bucking blood best.

The Buy-In

$20 per person. 8 players = $160 pot.

The Draft

  • 13 colts, 8 people.
  • Order determined by random draw.
  • Picks 1 through 8 each select a colt.
  • Remaining colts: Auction if any takers $2 increments. Buyer chooses from any of the remaining and can take as many as wanted for the same price.

The Scoring

  • Standard horse judging: 125 points
  • Will average scores from anyone that wants to (can do live or video replay)
  • Do we mark them live in the arena after each horse goes, or do it on TV after?
  • Your team score = sum of your colts' scores.
